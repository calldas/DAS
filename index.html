About

Born and raised in Boston, Caye became a talented multi-instrumentalist at an early age. His fondness for a variety of genres (from Jazz to Hip-Hop and everything in between) melded with his skills on the drums, steel pan, guitar, and piano. This set the stage for the essence of his music: smooth verses and seductive production.
